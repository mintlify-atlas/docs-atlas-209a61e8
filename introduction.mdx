# Welcome to AniDojo

AniDojo is a full-stack anime tracking, discovery, and review platform that helps you organize your anime journey, discover new series, and connect with fellow anime fans.

Built with modern web technologies including Next.js 15, React 19, Supabase, and the Jikan API (MyAnimeList), AniDojo provides a seamless experience for managing your anime collection and sharing your thoughts with the community.

## Platform Highlights

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="List Management">
    ### Powerful Anime Organization
    
    - **Multiple List Views**: Switch between card, table, and compact views
    - **Status Tracking**: Watching, Completed, Plan to Watch, On Hold, Dropped
    - **Episode Progress**: Track episodes watched with progress bars
    - **Rating System**: 10-point rating scale with star visualization
    - **Custom Lists**: Create and share custom anime collections
    - **Tags & Notes**: Add personal notes and custom tags to entries
    - **Import/Export**: Backup your lists or migrate from other platforms
    
    View your list statistics including total anime, episodes watched, mean score, and genre distribution.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Discovery">
    ### Find Your Next Anime
    
    - **Search**: Powerful search across 20,000+ anime titles
    - **Browse Top Anime**: Explore highest-rated series of all time
    - **Current Season**: Stay updated with ongoing releases
    - **Trending**: See what's popular in the community
    - **Filters**: Search by genre, year, type, and status
    - **AI Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions based on your taste
    
    All anime data is sourced from MyAnimeList through the Jikan API.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Reviews">
    ### Share Your Thoughts
    
    - **Multi-dimensional Ratings**: Rate Story, Animation, Sound, Character, and Enjoyment separately
    - **Rich Text Reviews**: Write detailed reviews with spoiler warnings
    - **Pros & Cons**: Structure your review with clear strengths and weaknesses
    - **Recommendations**: Add recommendation levels (Highly Recommend to Strongly Not Recommend)
    - **Draft System**: Save drafts and publish when ready
    - **Community Voting**: Mark reviews as helpful or unhelpful
    - **Comments**: Engage in discussions on reviews
    
    One published review per anime per user ensures quality content.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Community">
    ### Connect With Fans
    
    - **Public Profiles**: Share your anime journey with custom bios and avatars
    - **Public Lists**: Make your lists visible to the community
    - **Review Feed**: Discover what others are watching and reviewing
    - **Voting System**: Help surface the best reviews
    - **Comments & Replies**: Engage in threaded discussions
    
    Row Level Security ensures your data privacy while enabling community features.

## Database Schema

AniDojo uses a comprehensive PostgreSQL schema with the following tables:

- **profiles**: User profiles extending Supabase Auth
- **anime_entries**: User's anime lists with tracking data
- **reviews**: Detailed anime reviews with multi-dimensional ratings
- **review_votes**: Helpful/unhelpful votes on reviews
- **comments**: Threaded comments on reviews
- **comment_votes**: Upvote/downvote on comments
- **custom_lists**: User-created custom lists
- **custom_list_entries**: Anime entries in custom lists

All tables have Row Level Security policies to protect user data.
