## Overview

Reviews are at the heart of AniDojo. Share your thoughts, help others discover great anime, and contribute to the community. This guide covers everything from writing your first review to managing published reviews.

## Accessing Your Reviews

View all your reviews by navigating to **My Reviews** in the sidebar or going to `/my-reviews`.

```
Home › My Reviews
```

## Creating a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Anime">
    Navigate to the anime detail page by:
    - Browsing or searching for the anime
    - Clicking on it from your lists
    - Using the dashboard recommendations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Your Review">
    On the anime detail page, click the **Write Review** button. This opens the review editor.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Your Overall Rating">
    Rate the anime from 1-10 stars. This is your overall score that appears prominently with your review.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Your Review Title">
    Create a compelling title that summarizes your opinion (e.g., "A Masterpiece of Storytelling" or "Great Animation, Weak Plot").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write the Review Body">
    Share your detailed thoughts in the main text area. Consider discussing:
    - Story and plot
    - Character development
    - Animation quality
    - Sound and music
    - Overall enjoyment
    - Who would enjoy this anime
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Take your time writing reviews! Detailed, thoughtful reviews are more helpful to the community.
</Tip>

## Rating Categories

AniDojo supports detailed category ratings to help readers understand your opinion:

### Category Breakdown

- **Story Rating**: Plot, pacing, narrative structure
- **Animation Rating**: Visual quality, art style, animation fluidity
- **Sound Rating**: Voice acting, music, sound effects
- **Character Rating**: Character development, design, relationships
- **Enjoyment Rating**: Personal entertainment value

Each category can be rated independently from 1-10.

<Info>
Category ratings help readers quickly understand which aspects of the anime excel or fall short.
</Info>

## Review Sections

### Main Review Body

Your primary review text where you share detailed thoughts. Best practices:

- **Be specific**: Mention particular scenes, episodes, or characters
- **Support your opinions**: Explain why you felt a certain way
- **Avoid spoilers**: Keep major plot points hidden (or use spoiler tags)
- **Be constructive**: Even negative reviews can be helpful and respectful

### Pros and Cons

Quickly summarize strengths and weaknesses:

**Pros Field**:
- List what the anime does well
- Highlight standout features
- Mention memorable moments

Example: "Stunning animation, compelling protagonist, excellent soundtrack"

**Cons Field**:
- Note areas for improvement
- Mention disappointing aspects
- Point out potential issues

Example: "Slow pacing in middle episodes, underdeveloped side characters"

<Note>
Pros and cons appear as quick-reference sections at the bottom of your review.
</Note>

## Additional Review Features

### Spoiler Warnings

Toggle the **Contains Spoilers** option if your review discusses:
- Major plot twists
- Character deaths
- Ending details
- Significant story revelations

Reviews marked with spoilers display a warning to readers.

### Watch Status

Indicate your watching status:
- **Completed**: Watched all episodes
- **Watching**: Currently watching
- **Paused**: On hold
- **Dropped**: Stopped watching

This context helps readers understand your perspective.

### Episodes Watched

Specify how many episodes you watched:
- Shows you completed the series
- Explains if review is based on partial viewing
- Helps readers gauge review credibility

### Tags

Add relevant tags to your review:
- Genre tags ("Action", "Romance")
- Theme tags ("Character Study", "Plot Twist")
- Tone tags ("Dark", "Wholesome")

Tags help others find your review and understand content.

### Recommendation

Select your recommendation level:
- **Highly Recommended**: Must-watch anime
- **Recommended**: Worth watching
- **Mixed Feelings**: Has strengths and weaknesses
- **Not Recommended**: Skip it

This appears as a badge on your review.

## Publishing Your Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Content">
    Read through your review to check for:
    - Spelling and grammar
    - Spoilers that should be marked
    - Accuracy of information
    - Tone and clarity
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Status">
    Select publication status:
    - **Published**: Visible to the community immediately
    - **Draft**: Save for later editing
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the **Publish Review** or **Save Draft** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Published reviews appear on the anime's detail page and your profile. Drafts remain private until you publish them.
</Info>

## Managing Your Reviews

### Viewing Your Reviews

On the My Reviews page, you'll see:
- **Anime thumbnail** and title
- **Review title**
- **Your rating** (star display)
- **Publication date**
- **Helpful votes** count (for published reviews)
- **Review body** preview

### Editing Reviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Review Options">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on any review card.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Edit">
    Choose **Edit** from the dropdown menu. This returns you to the review editor.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make Changes">
    Update any aspect of your review:
    - Ratings
    - Title
    - Body text
    - Tags
    - Status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Updates">
    Click **Update Review** to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Edited reviews show the updated date. Consider noting major edits in your review text.
</Note>

### Deleting Reviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Review Menu">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on the review you want to delete.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Delete">
    Choose **Delete** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm that you want to permanently delete the review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Deleting a review is permanent and cannot be undone. The review will be removed from the anime page and your profile.
</Note>

### Sharing Reviews

Share your reviews with others:

1. Click the three-dot menu (⋯)
2. Select **Share**
3. The anime page URL is copied to your clipboard
4. Share the link on social media or with friends

## Community Interaction

### Helpful Votes

Other users can mark your reviews as helpful:
- Helpful count appears on your review
- Helps surface quality reviews
- Shows community appreciation

### Review Visibility

Published reviews appear:
- On the anime's detail page
- In your public profile (if enabled)
- In community review feeds
- In search results

<Tip>
Helpful, detailed reviews that avoid spoilers tend to receive more positive engagement!
</Tip>

## Writing Great Reviews

### Do's

✅ **Be honest** - Share your genuine opinion
✅ **Be specific** - Cite examples from the anime
✅ **Be balanced** - Acknowledge both strengths and weaknesses
✅ **Be respectful** - Respect others' opinions
✅ **Be helpful** - Help readers decide if they'd enjoy it
✅ **Proofread** - Check spelling and grammar

### Don'ts

❌ **Don't spoil** - Mark spoilers or avoid them
❌ **Don't attack** - No personal attacks on creators or fans
❌ **Don't spam** - One review per anime
❌ **Don't plagiarize** - Write original content
❌ **Don't rate without watching** - Base reviews on actual viewing

## Review Template

Here's a helpful structure for comprehensive reviews:

```
[Opening] - Brief overall impression

[Story/Plot] - Discuss narrative, pacing, themes

[Characters] - Analyze main and supporting characters

[Animation/Art] - Comment on visual quality

[Sound/Music] - Discuss soundtrack and voice acting

[Enjoyment] - Share personal experience

[Recommendation] - Who should watch this?

[Closing] - Final thoughts
```

<Tip>
You don't need to follow this template exactly - use it as a starting point and write in your natural voice!
</Tip>

## Tips for Review Success

1. **Watch the full anime** - Complete it before reviewing when possible
2. **Take notes** - Jot down thoughts while watching
3. **Wait a day** - Let your thoughts settle before writing
4. **Read other reviews** - See different perspectives (after watching)
5. **Update old reviews** - Your opinion may evolve over time
6. **Engage respectfully** - Respond to comments professionally
7. **Use all rating categories** - Provide comprehensive feedback
