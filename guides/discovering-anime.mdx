## Overview

AniDojo's AI-powered Discover feature helps you find anime based on your current mood, preferences, and watching history. Get personalized recommendations that match exactly how you're feeling right now.

## Accessing the Discover Page

Navigate to the Discover page by:
- Clicking **AI Recommender** or **Discover** in the sidebar
- Going to `/discover`
- Clicking the **DISCOVER ANIME** button on your dashboard

```
Home › Discover
```

<Info>
The Discover page is powered by AI that learns from your reviews, ratings, and anime lists to provide personalized recommendations.
</Info>

## How It Works

The recommendation system considers:

1. **Your mood selection** - How you're feeling right now
2. **Your anime history** - Based on your reviews and lists
3. **Advanced filters** - Your specific preferences
4. **Community data** - Popular and highly-rated anime
5. **Genre matching** - Aligned with your preferences

## Getting Recommendations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Mood">
    Click on one or more mood cards that match how you're feeling. Selected moods highlight with a red border.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Advanced Filters (Optional)">
    Click **Advanced Filters** to refine recommendations:
    - Specific genres
    - Anime type (TV, Movie, OVA)
    - Score range
    - Year range
    - Episode count
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Preferences">
    Toggle preference options:
    - Show anime you've already reviewed
    - Show anime in your lists
    - Prioritize highly rated anime
    - Include less popular anime
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Recommendations">
    Click the **Get Recommendations** button. The AI processes your mood, filters, and history.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Results">
    Browse your personalized recommendations:
    - Top pick highlighted at the top
    - Additional recommendations below
    - Match percentages shown
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Recommendations

### Top Pick

Your #1 recommendation is featured prominently:

**Displays**:
- Large cover image
- Title and basic info
- Star rating and type
- Genre tags
- Personalized explanation
- Match percentage

**Actions**:
- **Add to List** - Quickly add to your collection
- **Write Review** - Start a review immediately
- **View Details** - See full anime information

<Note>
The top pick is selected based on the highest match percentage and quality score.
</Note>

### Additional Recommendations

More anime that fit your criteria:

**Each card shows**:
- Cover art
- Title
- Star rating
- Match percentage (how well it fits your preferences)
- Genre tags
- Brief synopsis
- Quick actions

**Match Percentage**:
- 90-100%: Excellent match
- 75-89%: Great match
- 60-74%: Good match
- Below 60%: Moderate match

<Tip>
Higher match percentages indicate anime that closely align with your selected mood and preferences!
</Tip>

## Advanced Filters

Refine recommendations with detailed filters:

### Genre Selection

Choose specific genres:
- Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy
- Horror, Mystery, Romance, Sci-Fi, Slice of Life
- Supernatural, Psychological, Thriller
- And many more...

**How it works**:
- Select multiple genres for hybrid recommendations
- Overrides default mood genres
- More specific = more targeted results

### Type Filter

Narrow by anime format:
- **TV**: Full television series
- **Movie**: Feature-length films
- **OVA**: Original Video Animation
- **Special**: Special episodes
- **ONA**: Original Net Animation

<Info>
Select multiple types to get varied recommendations across different formats.
</Info>

### Score Range

Filter by quality:
- **Minimum Score**: Lower bound (1-10)
- **Maximum Score**: Upper bound (1-10)

Example: Set 7-10 to only see highly-rated anime.

### Year Range

Filter by release year:
- **From**: Earliest year (1960-2025)
- **To**: Latest year (1960-2025)

Examples:
- 2020-2025: Recent anime only
- 1990-2000: Nostalgic classics
- Any range: Explore different eras

<Tip>
Combine year range with "Nostalgic" mood to discover classics from specific decades!
</Tip>

## Recommendation Preferences

Customize how recommendations are generated:

### Show Reviewed Anime

**Enabled**: Includes anime you've already reviewed
**Disabled**: Excludes anime you've reviewed

Use case: Enable if you want to rewatch or revisit anime you've already seen.

### Show Anime in Lists

**Enabled**: Includes anime from your lists
**Disabled**: Shows only new anime

Use case: Disable to discover completely new titles.

### Prioritize Highly Rated

**Enabled**: Favors critically acclaimed anime
**Disabled**: More balanced mix of ratings

Use case: Enable for safer, proven quality choices.

### Include Less Popular

**Enabled**: Includes hidden gems and lesser-known titles
**Disabled**: Focuses on popular anime

Use case: Enable to discover underrated anime the community might have missed.

<Note>
Preferences work together to fine-tune your recommendation experience.
</Note>

### Save Recommendation Sets

Preserve great recommendation lists:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Recommendations">
    Get a set of recommendations you want to save.
  </Step>

## Understanding Match Percentages

How match percentages are calculated:

**Factors considered**:
- Genre overlap with mood (40%)
- Score range match (20%)
- Year range match (20%)
- Type preference match (10%)
- Personal history alignment (10%)

**Interpretation**:
- 95-100%: Perfect match for your criteria
- 85-94%: Excellent match, highly recommended
- 75-84%: Very good match
- 65-74%: Good match, worth considering
- Below 65%: Moderate match, might surprise you

## Troubleshooting

### No Recommendations Found

**Try**:
- Broadening your filters (wider score/year ranges)
- Selecting different moods
- Disabling some preferences
- Removing genre restrictions

### Recommendations Don't Match

**Check**:
- Selected moods are correct
- Advanced filters aren't too restrictive
- Preferences align with your goals

### Same Recommendations Repeatedly

**Solution**:
- Enable "Include less popular"
- Try different mood combinations
- Adjust genre filters
- Clear recommendation history
