## Overview

Your AniDojo profile is your identity in the anime community. Customize your profile, control privacy settings, and showcase your anime journey to other users.

## Accessing Your Profile

Navigate to your profile by:
- Clicking **Profile** in the sidebar
- Going to `/profile`
- Clicking your avatar in the navigation bar

```
Home › Profile
```

## Profile Overview

Your profile displays key information about your anime journey:

### Header Section

**Cover Image**
- Large banner at the top of your profile
- Personalizes your profile page
- Visible to all visitors

**Avatar**
- Profile picture displayed as a circular image
- Appears throughout the site
- Shows on your reviews and comments

**Display Name**
- Your visible name on AniDojo
- Shows on all your content
- Can be different from your username

**Username**
- Unique identifier (@username)
- Used in profile URLs
- Used for mentions and searches

<Tip>
Choose a display name that represents you in the anime community!
</Tip>

## Profile Statistics

Your profile prominently displays four key statistics:

### Anime Watched
Total number of anime titles in your lists across all statuses.

### Episodes
Total episodes watched across all anime in your collection.

### Days Watched
Estimated time spent watching anime (calculated at 24 minutes per episode).

### Mean Score
Your average rating across all anime you've scored.

<Info>
These statistics update automatically as you add anime to your lists and rate titles.
</Info>

## Profile Tabs

Your profile is organized into five main sections:

### Overview Tab

The default view showcasing:

**Recent Activity**
- Your latest anime activities
- Episode updates
- Reviews posted
- Ratings added
- List changes

**Currently Watching**
- Anime with "Watching" status
- Progress bars for each title
- Quick access to continue watching

**Favorite Anime**
- Your highest-rated anime (9+ scores)
- Visual grid of cover art
- Links to anime detail pages

**Quick Stats Widget**
- Anime by status breakdown
- Visual progress bars
- Status distribution chart
- Mean score display

### Anime Lists Tab

Full view of your anime collection:

**Status Filters**
- All, Watching, Completed, On Hold, Dropped, Plan to Watch
- Click to filter by status
- Shows count for each category

**View Modes**
- Grid: Visual card layout
- Table: Detailed spreadsheet view

See the [Creating Lists guide](/guides/creating-lists) for detailed list features.

### Reviews Tab

All your published reviews:

**Review Cards** display:
- Anime title and image
- Your star rating
- Review text preview
- Publication date
- Helpful votes count
- Quick actions (Edit, Delete)

**Filtering Options**:
- All Ratings
- 5 Stars
- 4 Stars
- 3 Stars
- 2 Stars
- 1 Star

### Stats Tab

Detailed analytics about your anime watching:

**Overview Statistics**
- Total Anime count
- Episodes Watched
- Days Watched
- Mean Score

**Score Distribution Chart**
- Bar chart showing ratings 1-10
- Visualizes your rating patterns
- Helps identify rating tendencies

**Top Genres Pie Chart**
- Shows your most-watched genres
- Percentage breakdown
- Color-coded visualization

<Note>
Charts and graphs update in real-time as you add and rate anime.
</Note>

### Favorites Tab

Curated showcase of your favorites:

**Favorite Anime**
- Anime you've marked with 9+ ratings
- Grid layout with cover art
- Edit to customize your favorites

**Future Features** (Coming Soon):
- Favorite Characters
- Favorite Voice Actors
- Favorite Studios

## Editing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile Editor">
    Click the **Edit Profile** button on your profile page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Basic Information">
    Modify your profile details:
    - Display Name
    - Bio (personal description)
    - Location
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Social Links">
    Connect your social accounts:
    - Twitter handle
    - Discord username
    - Personal website URL
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Images">
    Update your profile visuals:
    - Profile avatar (recommended: 150x150px)
    - Cover image (recommended: 1200x300px)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Profile** to apply your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use a clear, recognizable avatar so community members can identify you easily!
</Tip>

## Profile Customization

### Bio Section

Your bio appears below your username and stats:

**Best Practices**:
- Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
- Share your anime interests
- Mention favorite genres or shows
- Set expectations for your reviews

Example: "Passionate anime enthusiast who loves discovering new series and sharing reviews with the community."

### Member Since

Automatically displays your account creation date:
- Shows as "Member since Month Year"
- Cannot be edited
- Indicates your community tenure

### Location

Optional location field:
- City, Country format
- Helps connect with local fans
- Can be omitted for privacy

### Social Links

Connect with the community outside AniDojo:

**Twitter Integration**
- Add your @username
- Links to your Twitter profile
- Share anime thoughts across platforms

**Discord Username**
- Display your Discord ID
- Format: username#1234
- Connect for real-time chat

**Personal Website**
- Add your blog or website URL
- Share your anime content
- Build your online presence

<Info>
All social links are optional and can be hidden in your privacy settings.
</Info>

## Privacy Settings

Control what information is public:

### Public Profile

Toggle whether your profile is visible to:
- Registered users only
- Public (anyone on the internet)

### Review Visibility

Control review display:
- Show all reviews publicly
- Hide reviews from profile
- Reviews still appear on anime pages

### List Privacy

Configure anime list visibility:
- Public: Anyone can view
- Registered users only
- Private: Only you can see

### Activity Feed

Control activity visibility:
- Show recent activity to all
- Hide from non-followers
- Completely private

<Note>
Changing privacy settings takes effect immediately. Review settings carefully before saving.
</Note>

## Profile URLs

Your profile is accessible at:
```
https://anidojo.com/profile/[your-username]
```

Share this link to:
- Connect with friends
- Share on social media
- Link from other sites
- Display on streaming profiles

<Tip>
Customize your username early - changing it later affects your profile URL!
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

### Profile Not Updating
- Clear browser cache
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Try logging out and back in

### Image Upload Issues
- Ensure image is under 5MB
- Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, WebP)
- Check image dimensions
- Try a different browser

### Privacy Settings Not Saving
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Check form validation errors
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Contact support if issues persist
