## Overview

AniDojo provides multiple ways to discover and explore anime. Whether you're looking for popular titles, searching for something specific, or exploring by genre, this guide will help you find your next favorite anime.

## Browsing Top Anime

The Browse page displays the highest-rated anime of all time, making it easy to discover critically acclaimed series.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Browse">
    From your dashboard, click **Browse** in the sidebar or navigate to `/browse` from any page using the breadcrumb navigation:
    
    ```
    Home › Browse
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore the Anime Grid">
    The Browse page displays anime in a grid layout with:
    - **Cover art** for visual browsing
    - **Rank badges** showing position in top anime
    - **Title** and **year** information
    - **Genre tags** for quick reference
    
    Hover over any anime card to see the zoom animation effect.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Anime Details">
    Click on any anime card to navigate to its detailed page where you can:
    - Read the synopsis
    - View ratings and reviews
    - Add to your lists
    - Write a review
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate Through Pages">
    Use the pagination controls at the bottom:
    - **PREVIOUS** button to go back
    - **PAGE number** indicator shows current page
    - **NEXT** button to see more anime
    
    Each page displays 25 anime titles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The Browse page is perfect for discovering classic and highly-rated anime you might have missed!
</Tip>

## Using Global Search

AniDojo includes a powerful search feature available throughout the application.

### Accessing Search

The global search bar is located in the navigation bar at the top of every page. On desktop, it's visible in the center of the navigation; on mobile, access it through the menu.

### Search Features

- **Real-time search** - Results appear as you type
- **Title matching** - Searches both English and Japanese titles
- **Quick navigation** - Click any result to go directly to that anime's page

<Note>
The search function queries the MyAnimeList database, giving you access to thousands of anime titles.
</Note>

## Dashboard Discovery

Your dashboard provides personalized anime discovery:

### Recommended for You

AI-powered recommendations based on:
- Your anime reviews
- Lists you've created
- Your rating patterns
- Genres you enjoy

<Info>
The more you use AniDojo by adding anime to your lists and writing reviews, the better your recommendations become!
</Info>

### Trending This Week

Discover what's popular in the anime community:
- **Numbered rankings** show trending position
- Updates based on community popularity
- Mix of new and classic titles

### Current Season

Explore anime from the current broadcast season:
- Recently aired episodes
- New series premieres
- Ongoing shows

## Filtering and Sorting

While browsing anime:

- **By Status**: Currently airing, finished, upcoming
- **By Type**: TV series, movies, OVAs, specials
- **By Genre**: Action, romance, comedy, and more
- **By Rating**: Score ranges and popularity

<Tip>
Use the AI Recommender (Discover page) for advanced filtering based on your current mood and preferences!
</Tip>

## Tips for Effective Browsing

1. **Start with Browse** to discover highly-rated classics
2. **Check your Dashboard** daily for personalized recommendations
3. **Use Search** when looking for something specific
4. **Explore Trending** to stay current with the community
5. **Try the Discover feature** for mood-based recommendations
