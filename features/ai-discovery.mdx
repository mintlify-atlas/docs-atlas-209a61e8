## Overview

AniDojo's AI Discovery system analyzes your mood, preferences, and watching history to recommend anime that perfectly match what you're looking for right now. Tell us how you're feeling, and we'll find the perfect anime for you.

<Info>
  Recommendations combine mood-based matching with your personal ratings and watch history for highly personalized suggestions.
</Info>

### Match Breakdown

**90-100%**: Perfect match
- All or most genres align with your mood
- Meets all filter criteria
- High MAL score (if prioritized)

**70-89%**: Great match
- Strong genre overlap
- Meets most filter criteria
- Good MAL score

**50-69%**: Good match
- Some genre overlap
- Partially meets filters
- Acceptable score

**Below 50%**: Okay match
- Minimal genre alignment
- May not meet some filters
- Lower score or popularity

<Info>
  Match percentage helps you prioritize which anime to watch first. Start with 90%+ matches for best results!
</Info>

### Type Filter

Narrow by format:
- **TV**: Standard TV series (12-24 episodes)
- **Movie**: Anime films (1-3 hours)
- **OVA**: Original Video Animation (special episodes)
- **ONA**: Online-exclusive releases
- **Special**: One-off specials or extras

<Tip>
  Select "Movie" when you want a complete story in one sitting.
</Tip>

### Score Range

Set minimum/maximum MAL scores:

```
Score Range: [7.0] ─────●─── [10.0]
```

<Tip>
  Set minimum to 7.0+ for consistently good recommendations. Lower it to 5.0 for more variety and hidden gems.
</Tip>

### Year Range

Filter by release year:

```
Year Range: [2000] ─────●──────●───── [2024]
```

<Tip>
  Narrow to recent years (2020-2024) for modern animation quality. Expand to 1990+ for classic recommendations.
</Tip>

## Preference Toggles

### Show Reviewed Anime

**Enabled**: Include anime you've already reviewed
**Disabled**: Only show unreviewed anime

<Note>
  Useful if you want to discover entirely new anime vs. revisit shows you've seen.
</Note>

### Show Anime in Lists

**Enabled**: Include anime from your lists
**Disabled**: Only show anime not in your lists

<Tip>
  Disable to discover anime you haven't added yet. Enable if you want recommendations from your watchlist.
</Tip>

### Prioritize Highly Rated

**Enabled**: Weight toward high MAL scores
**Disabled**: Equal weighting regardless of score

<Info>
  Enable for "safe" recommendations with proven quality. Disable for more variety.
</Info>

### Include Less Popular

**Enabled**: Include under-the-radar anime
**Disabled**: Focus on mainstream titles

<Tip>
  Enable to discover hidden gems and niche anime. Disable for well-known, broadly appealing shows.
</Tip>

## How AI Learns

Recommendations improve as you use AniDojo:

1. **Rating History**: Your scores train the algorithm on your preferences
2. **Watch Patterns**: Status changes (completed, dropped) indicate quality
3. **Genre Preferences**: Frequently watched genres get weighted higher
4. **Time Patterns**: Algorithm learns when you watch certain types
5. **Review Text**: Sentiment analysis from reviews (coming soon)

<Info>
  The more you use AniDojo (rating, reviewing, tracking), the more personalized your recommendations become.
</Info>

## Mood-Based vs. Traditional Search

### Use Mood Discovery When:
- You're not sure what to watch
- You want something matching your feelings
- You're open to surprises
- You want curated suggestions

### Use Traditional Search When:
- You know the specific anime you want
- You're looking for a particular title
- You need precise filters (studio, year, etc.)
- You're researching a specific genre

<Tip>
  Combine both! Use Discovery for new ideas, then Search to find similar anime.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<Note>
  **Q: Why don't I see anime I've already watched?**
  
  A: Toggle "Show anime in my lists" and "Show reviewed anime" to include watched anime in recommendations.
</Note>

<Note>
  **Q: How often should I use Discovery vs. Search?**
  
  A: Use Discovery when you want suggestions, Search when you know what you want. Both complement each other!
</Note>

<Note>
  **Q: Can I get recommendations without selecting a mood?**
  
  A: Mood selection is required. If unsure, try "Adventurous" for broad appeal, or select multiple moods.
</Note>

<Note>  
  **Q: Why are some recommendations low matches?**
  
  A: Strict filters can limit results. Try relaxing some filters or changing moods for better matches.
</Note>
