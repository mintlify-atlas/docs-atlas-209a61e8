## Overview

Your AniDojo profile is your personal anime hub, showcasing your watching history, reviews, statistics, and favorites. Share it with the community or keep it private—you're in control.

<Info>
  Profile data syncs across devices using Supabase, so your stats and lists are always up-to-date.
</Info>

### Anime Lists Tab

View and manage your entire anime collection:

#### Status Filters

Switch between list categories:
- **All** - Complete collection
- **Watching** - Currently watching
- **Completed** - Finished watching  
- **On Hold** - Temporarily paused
- **Dropped** - Discontinued
- **Plan to Watch** - Watchlist

Each tab shows a count badge.

#### View Modes

**Grid View**: Card layout with cover art, title, status badge, rating, and episode progress.

**Table View**: Detailed spreadsheet with columns:
- Anime (thumbnail + title)
- Status
- Rating (visual stars)
- Progress (X/Y episodes)
- Last Updated date
- Quick actions

<Tip>
  Table view is best for bulk editing and sorting large collections.
</Tip>

## Customization

### Edit Profile

Click "Edit Profile" to update:

1. **Display Name**: Your public display name
2. **Username**: Unique identifier (used in profile URL)
3. **Bio**: Short description about yourself and anime preferences
4. **Location**: City/country (optional)
5. **Avatar**: Profile picture (upload or URL)
6. **Cover Image**: Banner background (recommended: 1200×300px)

### Social Links

Connect your profiles:
- **Twitter**: @username
- **Discord**: username#1234
- **Website**: Personal site or blog URL

<Info>
  Social links appear as icons on your profile header and are clickable by visitors.
</Info>

- **Public**: Anyone can view

## Profile Stats Calculation

### Total Anime
Count of unique anime across all lists (excluding duplicates).

### Episodes Watched  
Sum of `episodesWatched` from all anime entries.

```typescript
// From ~/workspace/source/src/app/profile/page.tsx:155
stats: {
  totalAnime: 247,
  episodesWatched: 3847,
  daysWatched: 64.1,
  meanScore: 7.8
}
```

### Days Watched
Calculated as: `(episodes × 24 minutes) / 1,440 minutes per day`

### Mean Score
Average of all anime ratings (1-10 scale), excluding unrated anime.

<Note>
  Standard deviation is also calculated to show rating consistency.
</Note>
